How it Works

Your journey
from here to IIM.

A clear, four-step path from application to B-school admission — with our team by your side every step of the way.

01
Apply & Map
Pay ₹2 application fee. Take the IQ-based Entrance Mapping Test to identify your exact starting level across all skill dimensions.
02
Get Placed
Based on your Mapping Test results, you're placed in the right percentile batch. Classes begin in your tailored curriculum.
03
Learn & Test
Attend live interactive AI-powered classes. Take unlimited mock tests. Upgrade batches weekly as you improve. Repeat cycles until you hit target.
04
Ace & Admit
Score 700+ / 95%ile+. Our team then guides your entire B-school application — essays, SOP, LOR, interviews — until you get admitted.
